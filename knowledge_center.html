Mediation Networks for iOS

The ironSource Mediation platform supports interstitial and video ads from leading ad networks, equipped with smart loading, ad placement technology and ad delivery optimization. Our Mediation solution is a new monetization tool that enhances user experience, offers better control on ad performance and significantly increases revenue!

Before You Start
Make sure you have correctly integrated ironSource ’s Rewarded Video orInterstitial or Banner Mediation into your application. 
i OS 13 and XCode 11
You can learn more about adapters readiness for iOS 13 and XCode 11 here .

Integrate the ironSource Mediation Network Adapters

Below you can easily select the ad networks you ’re interested in integrating with ironSource Mediation and copy the Cocoapods Script to your code. If you ’d still like to use Manual Download to integrate our adapters, you can find all the necessary links and information to do so below

Simply choose your preferred ad networks, scroll down and copy the customized pod script to your podfile. Make sure to follow the ad network integration guides on the menu to your left to properly set up the ad networks on the ironSource dashboard.

It would just be extra work that has already been done for you!
